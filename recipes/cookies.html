Cookie recipe

Description

Get ready to bake the best cookies you'll ever taste! Each bite filled with the perfect balance of sweet, soft, and chewy! This recipe is quick and easy to follow, which makes it perfect for beginners! To get started, follow the instructions provided below.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake at 180 degrees C for about 10 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
